Our Philosophy

Ma: The Beauty of Negative Space

The Editorial Omakase isn't just about dining; it's a curated narrative. We believe in the "Silent Chef" approach—where the experience stays out of the way, allowing the purity of the fish and the warmth of the vinegared rice to speak.

Each morning, our chef hand-selects the catch from Tsukiji market arrivals, ensuring only the top 1% of seasonal offerings grace our Hinoki wood counter.
